# M5 Review Rollback Story

When provider-backed review or team-workflow proof degrades, rollback or
downgrade follows the row-specific posture recorded in the certification
packet:

- object-vocabulary regressions preserve provider/local truth separation and stop broader claims;
- mutation-review regressions preserve the local draft instead of assuming a provider write succeeded;
- scope-review regressions force a fresh scope computation before retry;
- browser-handoff regressions preserve the typed return anchor packet;
- deferred-publish regressions preserve the queued packet for retry, export, or discard; and
- reconciliation regressions preserve audit-only evidence while broad provider-backed claims narrow.
