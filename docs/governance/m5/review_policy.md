# M5 Review Policy Story

Provider-backed review and team-workflow claims are allowed only while the
current certification packet proves all of the following:

- provider-backed objects stay visibly distinct from local drafts, queued publishes, and stale imports;
- acting identity and effective scope remain inspectable before any provider-backed mutation;
- browser-only fallback remains typed and return-anchor safe;
- deferred publish preserves export-safe lineage across restart, reconnect, and support handoff; and
- external callbacks and imports mutate visible state only through typed, deduplicated reconciliation paths.

If provider authority, publish-later continuity, or reconciliation proof goes
stale, the relevant row narrows automatically and broader marketing must stop
until the evidence is refreshed.
