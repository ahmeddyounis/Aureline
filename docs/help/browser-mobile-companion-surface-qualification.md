# Browser And Mobile Companion Scope

Browser and mobile companions are scoped surfaces. Review, comments, selected light edits, notification triage, and desktop handoff may be available depending on the row. Terminal, debugger, unrestricted runtime, trust, merge/rebase, rollback, and approval actions require desktop or a fresh higher-authority flow.

When a companion surface shows cached, stale, or offline state, treat it as non-live until it refreshes. `Open in desktop` preserves the exact workspace, object, tenant, route, return anchor, freshness, and expiry state, or opens a placeholder that explains why the target cannot be resumed yet.
