# Stabilize transport policy, proxy resolution, trust store, and mirror route

This stable packet makes enterprise transport behavior explainable across
update, marketplace, docs, AI, provider, remote, and bootstrap lanes. The
runtime truth source is
`aureline_policy::stabilize_transport_policy_proxy_resolution_trust_store_and_mirror_route`.

## Contract

The stable claim holds only when all of these are true:

1. Policy records and network events cover every required endpoint class:
   `update`, `marketplace`, `docs`, `ai`, `provider`, `remote`, and
   `bootstrap`.
2. Route-source precedence is exactly `policy_pinned`, `manual`, `system`,
   `pac`, `mirror_only`, and `offline`.
3. Every policy and event carries a proxy-resolution chain that selects exactly
   one effective route.
4. Trust-store snapshots preserve separate layers for OS roots, custom CA
   bundles, pinned SSH host proofs, client certificates, and imported mirror
   trust roots.
5. Policy block, contract mismatch, trust failure, transport failure,
   mirror route, and offline deferral use distinct tokens.
6. Control-plane and data-plane status remain separate on every policy and
   event.
7. Mirror/offline route state is present on every policy and event.
8. Local-core continuity is explicit on every policy record.
9. Raw credentials, bearer material, PAC bodies, certificate bodies, private
   keys, and raw host/URL/IP material are excluded from default records and
   support exports.
