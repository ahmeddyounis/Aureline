# Partner Benchmark Packet

This partner-facing packet projects the current `benchmark_publication`
posture without widening the claim. The canonical packet is
`artifacts/benchmarks/m3/publication_packet/packet.md`, and the council
disposition is `artifacts/benchmarks/m3/benchmark_council_notes.md`.

Current posture:

- `methodology-only`
- `not_yet_comparable`
- corpus and reference hardware metadata are publishable
- beta reference-workspace corpus rows are named in
  `artifacts/compat/m3/reference_workspace_register.yaml`
- beta reference-workspace report and badge rows are inspectable at
  `artifacts/compat/m3/reference_workspace_report.json` and
  `artifacts/compat/m3/reference_workspace_badges.json`
- thresholds and freshness/rerun triggers are inspectable
- public result-table copy is withheld until the required reruns complete

Partners can use this packet to inspect the beta benchmark method,
known limits, and rerun checklist. It is not a publishable numeric result
set.
